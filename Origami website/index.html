Interesting Facts about Pandas

  • Pandas have carnivorous teeth, but they eat bamboo and fruit.
  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  • Pandas live in bamboo forests as bamboo is thier main source of food.

Interesting Facts about Cicadas

  • The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all-male cicada chorus.
  • Most cicada have red-orange eyes.
  • Cicadas are medium to large in size, ranging from 2 to 5 cm (0.8 to 2 inches).

Interesting Facts about Chameleons

  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.
  • Their hands and feet have large toes that help them cling to branches.
  • Chameleons vary widly in terms of size.

Interesting Facts about Pigeons

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  • Pigeons are highly sociable animals.
